Strut your stuff

Feeling like a night out to strut your new garms?
We've got you covered.

Monday

FAC251 Quids in Monday!

Shake off those Monday blues with a trip to Fac251 Nightclub.

Tuesday

Jam Night at Element 19

Enjoy a night of Jam Music at Element 19. Free Food between 7pm - 9pm

Wednesday

QuiziModo

Wednesday nights at Trof NQ are quiz night. Assemble a team of trivial wizards and take on the Quizmodo masters, with free chips on offer for those that take part and funny team names as an essential.

Thursday

Bandaoke @Bunny Jackson's Joint

They say Thursday is the new Friday, and Bunny Jackson's is one of the best joints to be in come midweek partying. Expect one banging night of Bandaoke as you take to the stage like a legendary front man or woman and let it all out with a live band.

Friday

Top of the pops @The Mint Lounge

Taking over the Mint Lounge until the early hours, Top of the Pops is the Friday night bash that's all about cheap drinks and big tunes. A student-friendly club in Manchester, this buzzing night is always one to hit up.

Saturday

Cargo Manchester

You'll uncover Cargo nestled in The Printworks, fronting a pink bar space, a heaving light-up dancefloor and bottomless brunches aplenty when you want to start the party early. Plus, this late-night venue has plenty of budget-friendly tickets and student prices to boot.

Sunday

Bottomless Grazey Sunday - Cherry Jam

Cherry Jam on Every Sunday One of the best things to do in Manchester for couples on Sundays is Cherry Jam's Bottomless event. You'll get grazing boards accompanied by 90 minutes of bottomless cocktails, with free entry into musical bingo games for afters.
