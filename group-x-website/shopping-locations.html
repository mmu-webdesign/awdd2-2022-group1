Where to cop

These are the last of the fine brick and mortar shops in Manchester.

Some big brands, some indie names, always try and be sustainable when you're rocking your garms.

Bare Necessities

The best value vintage shop we've found on the map. Located up some stairs in between a subway and a boba tea shop on Oxford Road, you'll be trawling through the vintage gems in here for hours. A second location is being relocated after the sameful demolition plans of the market building opposite the Arndale. Img, Manchester Evening News, 2023: Online

Oxfam Originals

Buy some fresh garms and save the world. What's not to love? This NQ charity shop is perfect for finding extravagant clothes of all varieties. Lovely staff too! Img: Yelp, 2023: Online

Uniqlo

The place to grab all of your essentials at a decent price. Uniqlo has that perfect blend of affordability and NOT killing the planet with its fast fashon tendencies. Thanks, Uniqlo. Img: I Love Manchester, 2023: Online

Oi Polloi

A Northern Quarter institute. Liam Gallagher's favourite shop. Home of the designer. Oi Polloi has been serving us for years, and we thank it. Img: Highsnobiety, 2023: Online
